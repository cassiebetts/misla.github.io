ENTREPRENEUR CODING BOOTCAMP

Misla Coding Bootcamp

Code Your Own Website in 4 Weeks

MISLA’s Coding Bootcamp is designed specifically for entrepreneurs or employees to learn how to develop and maintain their own company websites. You will learn the tools and how to utilize industry hacks while coding to customize your own website. By the end of the Bootcamp you will have a final website published on the world wide web.

  • Learn basic understanding of HTML, CSS, & Javascript.
  • Easily edit text in html and add pictures to your website.
  • Learn how to customize your colors and fonts in CSS.
  • Program your site to accept payments for your products and services.
  • Learn to integrate with paypal’s merchant services.
  • Use Font Awesome to change icons quickly within the code.
  • Link your website to your social media pages or other online pages.

At MISLA, we are dedicated to increasing diversity in tech as well as providing economic growth within the community of South LA by empowering local startups as well as existing businesses to grow their digital presence. In partnership with VSEDC (Vermont Slauson Economic Development Corporation), we are able to award partial and full scholarships to lower income individuals with priority going to businesses of South LA. Please apply here for partial or full scholarship opportunities. Please note… Space is limited. We cannot guarantee your seat in the Bootcamp until your application is approved.
